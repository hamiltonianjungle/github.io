Ising Model

Key: IM16
Hamiltonian: The Transverse Ising model $$H=\displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}L_{ij}Z_iZ_j + \displaystyle\sum_{i\in\Lambda}f_iX_i$$
Problem: Ground state energy
Complexity: StoqMA–complete
Ref: [BH14]
Conditionals:
  • $L_{ij}$ and $f_i$ are real
  • $f_i \geq 0$
Reductions:
  • From the 2–local Hamiltonian
  • To the Ising model
Key: IM17
Hamiltonian: The Classical Ising model $$H=\displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}L_{ij}S_iS_j$$
Problem: Ground state energy
Complexity: NP–complete
Ref: [Bar82]
Conditionals:
  • $L_{ij}\in\{-1,0,1\}$
  • $S_i \in \{-1,1\}$
  • $\Lambda$ representing a $2$–level grid
Reductions:
  • From the TIM
